About me

I'm a researcher in the field of Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing and obtained my PhD in the field of Computational Semantics. I have published over 25 peer-reviewed papers in (among others) TACL, ACL, CL, EMNLP, COLING, EACL and IWCS across a diverse range of topics.

I'm an expert on ChatGPT and other Large Language Models, but I have also worked on semantic parsing and machine translation, all across multiple languages. I'm also interested in developing and evaluating resources for under-resourced European languages, as evidenced in my time working as a postdoctoral researcher in the MaCoCu project at the University of Groningen. Currently, I'm an Assistant Professor in Humane AI at the same university, specializing in the impact of ChatGPT on education and society.

I'm always open for collaborations (students, researchers, industry, anything), so just contact me if you want to have a chat!
