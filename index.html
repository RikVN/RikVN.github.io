About me

I'm a researcher in the field of Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing and obtained my PhD in the field of Computational Semantics. I have published over 25 peer-reviewed papers in (among others) TACL, ACL, CL, EMNLP, COLING, EACL and IWCS across a diverse range of topics.

My expertise lies mostly in semantic parsing, machine translation and language modelling, but I have also worked on author profiling, sentiment analysis and text classification, all across multiple languages. Currently I'm interested in developing and evaluating resources for under-resourced European languages, working as a postdoctoral researcher in the MaCoCu project at the University of Groningen.
